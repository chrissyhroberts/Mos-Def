Aim

To measure 12 immune markers in dried blood spot samples.

Important notes/Things that differ this time:

·         Biotin Ab aliquots allow for multichannel pipetting.

·         DBS rehydrated at 1 per 50 ul (2 in 100 ul) overnight.

·         Samples are run in SINGLE wells at each dilution.

All samples, standards & detection Abs are 30 ul per well volume.
