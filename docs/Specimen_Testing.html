Sample preparation in advance

  1. Dispense DBS samples into elution plates and record sample ID electronically to correspond with plate name and layout. Dispensed DBS can be sealed and stored as per the original sample storage so that they are ready to be tested in larger batches.

Day before assay day

  1. Check that MagPix machine is in working order and that calibration & verification kits and drive fluid are available.

  2. Prepare DBS elution buffer fresh on day of use:

Protease inhibitor: dissolve 1 tablet per 2 ml PBS to give 25X (Sigma, cOmplete, 04693116001)
